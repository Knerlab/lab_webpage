Dr. Peter Kner
Professor at University of Georgia
Research Interests
Optics, Biophotonics, Microscopy, Adaptive Optics, Super-Resolution, SIM, Non-Linear SIM, STORM, Holography
Education
Ph.D., Physics, UC Berkeley, 1998
M.S. in Physics, UC Berkeley, 1995
B.S. in Physics & EE, MIT, 1991
Bio
Dr. Kner joined UGA in 2009 from UCSF. He pioneered advanced microscopy systems and collaborated with Mats Gustafsson on the first live-cell SIM. At UGA, he is innovating novel microscopes to integrate multiple techniques for in vivo fluorescence imaging.
